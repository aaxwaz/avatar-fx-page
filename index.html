Avatar FX is a novel AI‑powered platform for interactive storytelling that lets users upload an image and choose a voice to instantly animate characters—speaking, moving, and expressing emotions with unprecedented realism. At its core is our state‑of‑the‑art DiT‑based diffusion video generation model, trained on a curated dataset and enhanced with innovative distillation and inference techniques to deliver high‑quality, temporally coherent videos at rapid speeds, even for extended sequences.
